Japanese Cheesecake

  • Back

Ingredients

  • 7 inch baking pan
  • 6 large eggs / 7 regular eggs
  • milk 60g 1/4 cup
  • butter 50g 1/4 cup
  • cream cheese 160g
  • cake flour 50g 1/3 cup (1 tablespoon for adjustment)
  • white vinegar 10g 2 teaspoon
  • sugar 75g 1/3 cup + 1 tablespoon

Recipe

  • combine the milk, butter, and cream cheese in a pan, whisk over a pot of boiling water so everything melts
  • separate the egg white and yolk, mix the yolk into the mixture after the mix cools down. If mix is too hot it will cook the egg
  • beat the egg/milk/butter/cream cheese mixture until bubbles form - need eggs to emulsify
  • sift flour into the egg mixture and then mix until the mixture is thick/li>
  • preheat oven to 320 F
  • add vinegar into the egg whites and beat until bubbles form
  • add sugar in 3-4 batches while mixing, add all the sugar in before soft peaks form
  • keep beating until soft peaks form then beat a little longer
  • fold egg white mixture into egg yolk mixture 3 times (fold not mix!)
  • pour into baking pan and hit the table a little bit to remove air bubbles
  • bake in water bath with aluminum foil hat for 1 hour at 300F
  • take off aluminum foil hat and bake for 20 minutes at 300F
  • let finished bake rest in the oven for 30 minutes before taking out, do not bang it on table!
  • let cool before serving
