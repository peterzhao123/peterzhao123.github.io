Ingredients

  • Genoise
  • 2 eggs
  • 55g sugar 1/4 cup
  • 10g honey 1/2 tbsp
  • 55g cake flour 1/2 cup
  • 10ml milk 2 teaspoon 1/2 tbsp
  • 10g unsalted butter 2 teaspoon
  • Syrup
  • 20g sugar 1.5 tbsp, 1/8 cup
  • 40ml hot water
  • 1 tbsp liquor
  • 400ml heavy cream
  • 35g sweet condensed milk 2 tbsp
  • strawberry

Recipe

  • Genoise
  • Preheat oven to 340F
  • Electric whisk 2 eggs until bubbly, add in sugar
  • Put egg+sugar mixture over hot pot of water and whisk until 35 degrees (hot)
  • Warm milk + butter over hot pot of water
  • Whisk egg+sugar mixture until light and creamy, should form folds when you lift it up
  • Pour in honey and whisk, pour in cake flour and fold into mixture (mix 30 - 35 times)
  • Put a little bit of mxiture into milk+ butter, then mix milk+butter into the mixture (mix 20-30 times)
  • Put in baking pan and bake for 25-30 minutes
  • Syrup
  • Mix together
  • Whipped Cream
  • Mix together whipping cream and condensed milk
  • Electric whisk over cold water until soft peaks then cool in fridge
  • Cut genoise into 3 layers, layer whipped cream and syrup on top of each other
