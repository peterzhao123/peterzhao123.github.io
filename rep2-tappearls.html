Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup water
  • 1 cup tapioca starch
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar

Recipe

  • Cook water and brown sugar in pan
  • When it starts to boil add in half of starch
  • When its sticky and water is gone, turn off the heat and add the rest of the starch
  • Pour onto table and knead until smooth
  • Roll thin and cut into small pieces
  • Roll into balls and cover in starch
  • Cook for 15 min in boiling water and let set for 5 mins
  • Strain and put in sugar water
